UI Designer

Credelite

Job Details

Salary

₱50,000 - ₱80,000

Job type

Full-time, Part-time, Internship, Remote

Roles and Responsibilities

Our society is facing an infodemic crisis. Today’s leading platforms were built to amplify popularity and are ill-equipped to uphold objective scrutiny and rational discourse. We can change that.

About Us

We are an early stage stealth startup committed to restoring the trustworthiness of information on the web. Backed by experienced entrepreneurs, designers, engineers, and data scientists, our platform combines novel approaches in machine learning, mechanism design, and behavioral design to augment human intelligence and enable real-time collaborative sourcing, organization, peer review, and verification of information at scale. We are a fully distributed team spanning North America, Europe, and Asia with our tech team based mostly in Singapore. Our team members hail from NTU, NUS, Stanford, and other top institutions.

Description

We are looking for a Product Designer with a passion for taking on complex problems to create simple, elegant solutions. You will collaborate with other designers and engineers to identify new opportunities, improve features, and assist in laying the foundations of design for our first product, all while anticipating users’ needs and advocating for them.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with user research efforts to identify potential target users and their needs.
  • Be the voice of the user. Listen to users and understand their pain points to identify the best strategies and solutions to improve the overall experience for them.
  • Brainstorm new concepts and produce wireframes and prototypes at appropriate levels of fidelity for demonstration.
  • Assist with user testing planning and execution. Validate concepts with users and incorporate their feedback into new design iterations.
  • Produce high-fidelity mockups and design assets for implementation that elegantly solve design problems and adhere to established visual design language and brand guidelines.
  • Raise the bar for UX/UI design excellence and help implement design best practices.

Qualifications

  • Strong interaction design fundamentals and ability to execute in a fast-paced environment.
  • Experience developing wireframes, user flows, prototypes, etc.
  • Passion for design and building great products.
  • Proficiency with design tools e.g. Figma, Sketch, Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Detail-oriented. You take pride in your craft and sweat the details.
  • Excellent communication skills. You should be able to justify design decisions and give and receive constructive feedback.
  • Flexible and adaptive to change in a startup environment.
  • Collaborative mindset. You work well in cross-functional teams.
  • Empathy for users. You are able to identify user pain points and pinpoint existing issues.
  • Bonus points for interest/experience in UX copywriting, branding, and designing marketing collateral (e.g. email).
To apply, please include a link to your portfolio with examples of recent design work showcasing your process and a short blurb on why you are interested in this role.
